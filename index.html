Hello, I am Leoto,

and this is 'Pure'

Introducing
Pure

The author, using his unique style and vision of electronic music itself, draws ubelievebly bright and clear images of loneliness, self-finding and soul emptiness.

 

This work hasn't any straight message - each person has it's own, absolutely individual point of view. The main aim of Leoto's 'Pure' is not in sermonizing, but in making a dialogue with his listener, in order to direct him to certain thoughts.

 

'Pure' album is absolutely clear. It refers not to the world, but person to itself.
